03 — The System
How DMX Works
No complex setup. No hardware beyond a basic Android phone for your waiter. No change to how your kitchen runs. DMX plugs into your existing workflow and makes it better.
01

Customer Scans QR at Their Table

A unique QR sticker sits on every table. Customer scans it with any phone — no app download, no login required. Your digital menu opens instantly in their browser. The table is automatically identified.

02

They Browse, Add to Cart, and Place Order

The menu shows live stock status, item descriptions, dietary tags, and any active discounts. Customer adds items to cart, adds special notes ("no onion", "extra spicy"), and places the order. The running bill is visible throughout.

03

Your Waiter Gets Buzzed Instantly

The moment the order is placed, your waiter's app gets a push notification — even if the phone is in their pocket. The notification shows the table number, item count, and a quick preview. Waiter taps to see the full order and heads to the kitchen as they always do. Your kitchen workflow stays exactly the same.

04

Food Served. More Orders Can Come In.

The customer can order more rounds throughout the session — each new order fires a fresh notification to the waiter. All items get added to one running bill for the table. No paperwork, no re-adding.

05

Bill Request — One Tap or Just Call Out

When the customer is done, they either tap the "Call Waiter" button in the app — or they just wave as usual. Both work. If they tap, your waiter gets an instant buzz notification showing the table number and the exact total already calculated. Waiter arrives prepared. No fumbling with a calculator.

06

Payment Collected — Your Way

Cash, UPI, card — whatever your restaurant uses. Waiter collects as normal, marks the table as settled on the app. The table is freed. Your dashboard updates in real time. That transaction's data feeds your analytics engine immediately.

07

Your Business Intelligence Updates

Every settled bill enriches your analytics. Which item sold the most today? Which table took the longest? What was your peak hour? What's your profit per dish? Your admin dashboard has the answer, updated after every single transaction.

09 — Common Questions
We've Heard Every Concern
Will my older customers be able to use a QR menu? They're not tech-savvy.
Your waiter can still take the order the traditional way for any customer who prefers it. DMX doesn't remove the human element — it enhances it. For the majority of your customers who do use it, it means faster service and fewer errors. The QR menu works on every smartphone, including basic Android phones, with no download or login. If a customer can send a WhatsApp, they can use DMX.
What if the internet goes down? My restaurant can't stop working.
The waiter app is built with offline capability — it queues notifications and syncs when the connection is restored. Your wifi going down does not shut down your restaurant. We also recommend pairing DMX with a basic mobile data SIM in the waiter device as a fallback. Additionally, waiters can always revert to taking orders manually for the duration of an outage — DMX is an enhancer, not a dependency.
We already have a system / POS. Why switch?
DMX is not a competitor to your existing POS — it's what sits on top of it. If you have a POS for billing, DMX handles the customer-facing ordering and gives you the analytics layer. Many restaurants use both. That said, DMX's billing module can replace a basic POS entirely for most restaurant types. We assess your current setup before recommending.
My staff will resist learning a new system.
The waiter app has exactly two actions: view the new order, and mark the table as settled. That's it. Training takes under 30 minutes. The customer app requires no training — customers figure it out on their own in under 60 seconds, the same way they figure out any other app. The admin dashboard is slightly more involved, but we walk you through it personally during onboarding.
